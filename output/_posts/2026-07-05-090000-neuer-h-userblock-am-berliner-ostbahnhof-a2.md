Am Berliner Ostbahnhof <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-2">entsteht</button> jetzt ein neuer <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-1">Häuserblock</button>. Er heißt B.YARD und <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-3">liegt</button> an der Straße der Pariser Kommune 20. Der <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-4">Bau</button> an diesem <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-5">Ort</button> hat diese Woche <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-6">begonnen</button>.

<button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-7">Bis Anfang</button> 2028 <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-9">sollen</button> <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-8">dort</button> Büros, 60 <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-10">Wohnungen</button>, <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-11">Läden</button> und Restaurants entstehen. <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-8">Dort</button> soll es auch <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-12">Räume</button> für Kunst geben. Das Projekt kostet <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-13">etwa</button> 90 Millionen Euro. DCD Services <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-14">plant</button> und <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-15">baut</button> das Projekt. Equora Capital <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-17">besitzt</button> das <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-16">Grundstück</button>.

Der neue <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-1">Häuserblock</button> soll einen <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-18">Hof</button> <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-19">in der Mitte</button> haben. <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-8">Dort</button> können Menschen aus den Häusern <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-20">sich treffen</button>. Auf den Dächern <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-9">sollen</button> Pflanzen <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-21">wachsen</button>. Auf dem <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-22">höchsten</button> Dach <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-9">sollen</button> <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-23">Anlagen</button> für <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-24">Strom</button> aus <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-25">Sonne</button> stehen. Unter dem Haus gibt es nur 17 <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-26">Plätze</button> für Autos. Das <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-27">passt</button> zum Ostbahnhof, <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-28">weil</button> <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-8">dort</button> viele <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-29">Züge</button> und Busse fahren.

Aber das <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-16">Grundstück</button> hat eine <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-30">schwierige Geschichte</button>. <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-8">Dort</button> <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-32">stand</button> <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-31">früher</button> ein <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-33">Wohnhaus</button> aus der <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-34">DDR-Zeit</button>. Für das neue Projekt <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-35">verschwand</button> das Haus im Jahr 2023. Seit dem Jahr 2021 <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-36">wohnte</button> <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-8">dort</button> niemand mehr. Vorher <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-37">lebten</button> <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-8">dort</button> <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-13">etwa</button> 350 Menschen, auch viele Roma-Familien.

Das alte Haus war viele Jahre in schlechtem <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-38">Zustand</button>. Einige <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-10">Wohnungen</button> waren <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-39">laut Amt</button> nicht mehr <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-40">sicher</button> zum Wohnen. Nach einem <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-41">Verkauf</button> im Jahr 2019 gab es viel <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-42">Streit</button>. Der <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-42">Streit</button> <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-43">ging um</button> <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-44">Reparaturen</button>, Mieten und Kündigungen. Gruppen von Mietern <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-45">kritisierten</button> den früheren <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-46">Eigentümer</button> stark. Heute ist <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-47">nicht ganz klar</button>, wo alle früheren Mieter leben. Einige waren <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-48">eine Zeit lang</button> in <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-49">Notunterkünften</button>. Andere frühere Mieter haben später neue <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-10">Wohnungen</button> <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-50">gefunden</button>.
