Berlin <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-1">hat seit</button> Mittwoch eine neue sehr lange <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-3">Straßenbahn</button> im normalen <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-2">Fahrgastbetrieb</button>. Der „Urbanliner“ fährt auf der Linie M4 <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-4">zwischen</button> Hackeschem Markt und Sulzfelder Straße. Die BVG <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-5">stellte</button> den Start nicht groß <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-6">öffentlich</button> vor. <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-7">Deshalb</button> <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-8">bemerkten</button> viele <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-9">Fahrgäste</button> die Premiere <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-10">kaum</button>, <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-11">obwohl</button> der <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-12">Zug</button> mehr als 50 Meter lang ist. <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-13">Besonders</button> in Kurven sieht man seine Größe. Vorn fährt er schon wieder <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-14">geradeaus</button>, <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-15">während</button> das <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-16">Ende</button> <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-54">noch</button> vor der Kurve ist.

Der <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-12">Zug</button> soll <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-17">vor allem</button> die <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-18">stark genutzte</button> M4 <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-19">entlasten</button>. Er <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-20">hat Platz</button> für 303 <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-9">Fahrgäste</button>: 88 Menschen können sitzen, 215 können stehen. <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-21">Damit</button> bietet er mehr <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-22">Raum</button> als <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-23">ältere</button> Bahnen, die dort oft als zwei <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-24">verbundene</button> <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-25">Wagen</button> fahren. Auch <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-26">innen</button> <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-27">wirkt</button> der Urbanliner <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-28">offen</button> und groß. <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-9">Fahrgäste</button> können durch den ganzen <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-12">Zug</button> gehen. <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-29">Fahrkartenautomaten</button> gibt es nicht mehr, aber <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-30">Entwerter</button> sind <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-31">vorhanden</button>. Die BVG <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-32">setzt stärker auf</button> digitale Tickets und <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-33">Verkaufsstellen</button> an der <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-34">Strecke</button>. In den Bahnen kaufen nur <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-54">noch</button> wenige Menschen Papierfahrscheine.

<button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-35">Bis</button> zur ersten Fahrt mit Fahrgästen <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-36">dauerte</button> es <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-37">jedoch</button> lange. Die BVG <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-38">bestellte</button> die neue Generation schon <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-16">Ende</button> 2020. Das erste <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-39">Fahrzeug</button> kam aber erst im Sommer 2024 nach Berlin. Ein Start im Februar <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-40">klappte</button> nicht, weil die <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-41">Genehmigung</button> <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-42">fehlte</button>. <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-43">Außerdem</button> musste die BVG technische <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-44">Nachweise</button> <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-45">nachreichen</button>. Auch das hohe <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-46">Gewicht</button> <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-47">machte Probleme</button> am Alexanderplatz. Leer <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-48">wiegt</button> der Urbanliner etwa 63 Tonnen, <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-49">voll</button> <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-35">bis</button> zu 100 Tonnen. <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-7">Deshalb</button> <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-50">verstärkte</button> man dort die <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-51">Tunnelanlagen</button>. <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-16">Ende</button> Juni gab die <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-52">Aufsichtsbehörde</button> den <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-53">Betrieb</button> mit Fahrgästen frei.

<button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-54">Noch</button> <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-55">darf</button> der <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-12">Zug</button> nur <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-56">eingeschränkt</button> fahren. Er muss <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-57">vorerst</button> mit einem <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-58">bestimmten</button> <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-59">Führerstand</button> <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-60">nach vorn</button> <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-61">unterwegs</button> sein. Auf der M4 ist das kein Problem, weil es dort <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-62">Wendeschleifen</button> gibt. Andere Linien haben solche <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-63">Anlagen</button> nicht immer. <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-64">Bis Jahresende</button> sollen etwa 15 Urbanliner fahren. Im Jahr 2028 könnten es 30 auf der M4 sein. <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-65">Insgesamt</button> hat Berlin 65 Züge bestellt. <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-66">Langfristig</button> sollen sie mehr Platz im <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-67">Straßenbahnnetz</button> <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-68">schaffen</button>, <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-11">obwohl</button> die <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-69">Einführung</button> <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-70">später</button> kam als <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-71">geplant</button>.
