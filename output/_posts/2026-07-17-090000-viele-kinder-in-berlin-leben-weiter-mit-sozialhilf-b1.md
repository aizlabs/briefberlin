Die Zahlen <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-4">beziehen sich auf</button> das <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-5">Jahresende</button> 2025. Zwischen den Berliner Bezirken bleibt <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-13">Kinderarmut</button> <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-14">unterschiedlich stark</button>. Viele <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-16">betroffene</button> Familien <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-17">erhalten</button> <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-18">staatliche</button> <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-19">Sozialleistungen</button>. Das ist Geld vom Staat, wenn das eigene <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-20">Einkommen</button> nicht zum Leben <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-21">reicht</button>. <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-22">Dazu gehören</button> zum Beispiel <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-23">Hilfen</button> für <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-24">Miete</button>, Essen und andere wichtige Kosten.

Die <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-25">Verwaltung</button> <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-26">zählt</button> viele dieser Familien als <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-27">Bedarfsgemeinschaften</button>. <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-28">Damit meint</button> sie <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-29">Haushalte</button>, in denen Menschen <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-30">zusammenleben</button> und <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-31">gemeinsam</button> <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-32">Unterstützung</button> bekommen. Zu diesen Haushalten gehören auch <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-2">Kinder und Jugendliche</button>. Ihre Zahl ist <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-33">zuletzt</button> <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-34">gesunken</button>. <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-35">Im Vergleich</button> zum <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-36">Vorjahr</button> gab es 4,7 Prozent weniger <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-37">Minderjährige</button> in <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-38">solchen</button> <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-27">Bedarfsgemeinschaften</button>.

<button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-39">Trotzdem</button> <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-41">bedeutet</button> dieser <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-40">Rückgang</button> nicht automatisch eine klare soziale <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-42">Verbesserung</button>. Denn <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-43">gleichzeitig</button> ist die Zahl <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-44">jüngerer</button> Kinder in Berlin <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-45">deutlich</button> <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-46">kleiner geworden</button>. Wenn es <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-10">insgesamt</button> weniger kleine Kinder gibt, <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-47">sinkt</button> auch die Zahl der betroffenen Kinder leichter. <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-48">Deshalb</button> muss man die <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-49">Entwicklung</button> <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-50">vorsichtig</button> lesen.

Die <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-51">Quote</button> bleibt <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-1">weiterhin</button> hoch. <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-52">Mehr als</button> <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-53">jedes fünfte</button> Kind in Berlin lebt in einer Familie, die auf <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-18">staatliche</button> Hilfe <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-54">angewiesen ist</button>.
