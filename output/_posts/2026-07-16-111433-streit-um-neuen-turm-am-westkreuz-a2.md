So kann es das <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-6">höchste</button> Haus in Berlin werden. Die <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-7">Besitzer</button> von Artemis in Halensee <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-8">wollen</button> den <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-9">Turm</button> bauen, und ihr Haus für Sex-Arbeit soll <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-10">umziehen</button>.

Das <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-11">Grundstück</button> <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-12">liegt</button> <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-13">nahe</button> an der Autobahn A100. Auch der <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-14">S-Bahnhof</button> Westkreuz und die <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-15">Messe</button> liegen <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-16">direkt daneben</button>. Die Planer <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-8">wollen</button> <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-26">Büros</button> und <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-27">Räume</button> für die <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-15">Messe</button> bauen. <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-33">Aber</button> viele <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-34">Fachleute</button> sehen die große <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-5">Höhe</button> kritisch. Sie <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-36">fragt</button>, ob so ein <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-9">Turm</button> zu <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-3">neuen</button> <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-37">Wohnungen</button> und <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-38">Arbeitsorten</button> dort <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-39">passt</button>. Der <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-40">alte</button> Funkturm ist 147 Meter hoch, und die Stadt <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-41">schützt</button> ihn. Kritiker sagen: Der neue <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-9">Turm</button> nimmt ihm viel <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-42">Wirkung</button>.
