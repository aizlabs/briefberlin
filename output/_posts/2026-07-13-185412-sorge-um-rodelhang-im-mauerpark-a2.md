Im Berliner Mauerpark gibt es <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-1">Sorge um</button> einen <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-3">Hügel</button>. Der <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-3">Hügel</button> heißt Sonnenhügel und <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-4">liegt</button> <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-5">neben</button> dem Jahn-Sportpark. Dort <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-35">arbeiten</button> Firmen <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-6">seit kurzer Zeit</button> am <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-7">Hang</button>.

Menschen <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-8">dürfen</button> im Moment nicht auf diesen <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-9">Teil</button> des Hangs gehen. Seit vielen Jahren <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-10">löst sich</button> dort <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-11">immer wieder</button> Erde. <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-12">Deshalb</button> ist der <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-13">Boden</button> an der <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-14">steilen</button> <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-15">Stelle</button> <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-16">unsicher</button>. Viele Familien fahren dort im Winter mit <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-17">Schlitten</button> den <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-7">Hang</button> <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-18">hinunter</button>. In der Innenstadt gibt es nur <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-19">wenige</button> solche Orte.

Der <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-3">Hügel</button> hat auch eine <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-20">besondere</button> <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-21">Geschichte</button> im Park. Im <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-3">Hügel</button> <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-4">liegt</button> <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-22">Schutt</button> aus <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-23">kaputten</button> <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-24">Häusern</button>. Dieser <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-22">Schutt</button> kommt aus der Zeit nach dem Zweiten Weltkrieg. <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-25">Früher</button> war dort die <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-26">Grenze</button> zwischen Ost- und West-Berlin. Heute malen viele Menschen Bilder auf eine alte <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-27">Mauer</button> am <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-7">Hang</button>.

Regen, Wind und Frost <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-28">verändern</button> den <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-7">Hang</button> immer mehr. Am Eschenhain rutscht Erde ab und <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-29">belastet</button> die Bäume. Die Firma Grün Berlin <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-30">kümmert sich</button> um viele Parks der Stadt. Sie will den <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-7">Hang</button> <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-31">sicherer</button> machen. <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-32">Außerdem</button> will sie stoppen, dass noch mehr Erde <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-33">wegrutscht</button>.

Noch gibt es keinen <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-34">festen Plan</button> für die <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-35">Arbeiten</button>. <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-36">Arbeiter</button> haben schon große <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-37">Steine</button> aus Granit <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-38">aufgestellt</button>. Sie haben auch Teile des Hangs für Tests <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-39">geöffnet</button>. <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-40">Fachleute</button> <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-41">prüfen</button> nun verschiedene <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-42">Lösungen</button>. Dabei denken sie an Sicherheit, Bäume und die <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-43">Nutzung</button> durch <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-44">Besucher</button>.

<button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-45">Gerade deshalb</button> <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-46">wächst</button> die <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-1">Sorge um</button> den <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-2">Rodelhang</button>. Kritiker wollen eine Lösung mit <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-47">Platz</button> für <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-17">Schlitten</button>. Grün Berlin will <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-48">später</button> mehr Informationen geben. Noch ist <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-49">offen</button>, ob Familien dort <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-50">weiter</button> <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-51">rodeln</button> können.
