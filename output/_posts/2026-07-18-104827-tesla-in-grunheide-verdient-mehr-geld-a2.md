Tesla hat ein großes <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-1">Werk</button> in Grünheide bei Berlin. <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-2">Dort</button> hat die <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-3">Firma</button> 2025 mehr <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-4">Gewinn</button> gemacht. Die <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-5">Nachfrage</button> nach Autos war aber <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-6">schwächer</button>.

Nach einem <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-7">Bericht</button> von Tesla <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-8">lag</button> der <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-4">Gewinn</button> bei 77,1 Millionen Euro. Ein Jahr <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-9">vorher</button> waren es 56,8 Millionen Euro. Der <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-10">Umsatz</button>, <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-11">also</button> das Geld aus Verkäufen, <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-12">sank</button> <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-13">etwas</button>. Er <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-12">sank</button> von 7,6 auf 7,1 <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-14">Milliarden</button> Euro.

Auch die Produktion war im Jahr 2025 <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-13">etwas</button> kleiner. Tesla <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-15">baute</button> <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-16">etwa</button> 202.000 Autos, <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-17">rund</button> 9.000 <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-18">weniger</button> als 2024. <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-19">Seit</button> 2022 hat Tesla <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-2">dort</button> <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-16">etwa</button> 700.000 Autos gebaut. Der <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-4">Gewinn</button> <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-20">stieg</button>, <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-21">weil</button> die <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-22">Kosten</button> beim <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-23">Bauen</button> <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-24">niedriger</button> waren.

Das <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-1">Werk</button> ist sehr <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-25">wichtig</button> für Tesla in Europa. Es baut <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-26">vor allem</button> das Model Y. Dieses Auto war 2025 das <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-27">meistverkaufte</button> Elektroauto in Europa. Grünheide <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-28">liefert</button> Autos in mehr als 30 <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-29">Länder</button> und Regionen.

Im <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-30">letzten Jahr</button> <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-15">baute</button> Tesla auch <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-31">Teile</button> der Fabrik um. <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-32">Danach</button> <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-33">konnte</button> das <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-1">Werk</button> neue Versionen des Model Y <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-23">bauen</button>. Es <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-15">baute</button> auch Autos für Kanada. Für 2026 <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-34">plant</button> Tesla in Brandenburg viel mehr Autos.

Der Staat <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-35">erlaubt</button> <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-36">bis zu</button> 500.000 Autos <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-37">pro Jahr</button>. Ab Juli will Tesla 6.200 Autos <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-38">pro Woche</button> <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-23">bauen</button>. <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-39">Außerdem</button> will Tesla viel Geld in Auto-Batterien <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-40">stecken</button>. <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-41">Ende</button> 2025 <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-42">arbeiteten</button> 11.083 <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-43">Menschen</button> im <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-1">Werk</button>.

Viele deutsche <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-44">Autofirmen</button> haben im Moment Probleme. Sie <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-45">verkaufen</button> <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-18">weniger</button> Autos und ihre <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-22">Kosten</button> sind <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-46">hoch</button>. Tesla <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-47">nennt</button> aber auch Risiken für sein <button type="button" class="article-term article-term--default" data-term-id="term-1">Werk</button>. Probleme mit <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-48">Lieferungen</button>, politische Krisen oder <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-49">schlechte</button> <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-50">Nachrichten</button> können dem <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-51">Geschäft</button> <button type="button" class="article-term" data-term-id="term-52">schaden</button>.

## Vokabeln

- **Werk** - factory; plant - Ort, wo man Dinge baut oder herstellt.
- **Gewinn** - profit - Geld, das nach den Kosten bleibt.
- **Nachfrage** - demand - Wie viele Menschen etwas kaufen wollen.
- **Umsatz** - revenue; sales - Geld, das eine Firma durch Verkauf bekommt.
- **sank** - fell; decreased - wurde kleiner oder weniger
- **stieg** - rose; increased - wurde größer oder mehr
- **Kosten** - costs - Geld, das man bezahlen muss.
