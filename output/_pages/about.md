## Our Mission

BriefBerlin makes Berlin's news accessible to people learning German, combining clear language with meaningful local context so readers can develop their language skills while staying connected to the city. Our broader goal is to help make the German language universally accessible and useful, giving learners language they can apply to understand and participate in the communities around them.

## Our Story

BriefBerlin was founded in June 2026 as an independent publication for readers who want to follow Berlin while developing their German. Our coverage focuses on the civic, cultural, social, and everyday stories that shape life in Berlin. We occasionally cover broader German news when it is especially relevant to our readers.

Our articles preserve the essential facts and context of current reporting while presenting them in language designed to be more approachable for learners.

## Ownership and Funding

BriefBerlin is owned and operated by **BriefBerlin LLC**, a company registered in Delaware, United States. The publication has no parent company.

BriefBerlin is independently owned and privately funded by its founder. It does not currently receive funding from advertisers, subscribers, grants, sponsors, or outside investors. Funding arrangements do not determine editorial coverage.
