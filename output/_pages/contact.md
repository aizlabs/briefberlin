## Email

For editorial questions, correction requests, rights inquiries, and general correspondence, email [info@briefberlin.de](mailto:info@briefberlin.de).

When reporting a possible error, please include the article title or URL, the information you believe is incorrect, and any supporting source you would like us to review.

See our [Corrections Policy]({{ '/corrections/' | relative_url }}) for information about how reports are reviewed and how substantive changes are disclosed.
