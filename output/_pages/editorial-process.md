## Author-Led Production

BriefBerlin follows an author-led, technology-assisted editorial process. Authors select the source material, determine the editorial focus, verify the facts and source fidelity, edit the language and headline, and take responsibility for the final publication.

Automated language tools may assist with drafting, language adaptation, vocabulary support, and audio production. They do not decide what BriefBerlin covers or publish content independently. Every article is reviewed, edited, and approved by its named author.

## Human Review

Before publication, the author reviews factual accuracy, source fidelity, headline accuracy, German-language quality, suitability for language learners, and legal or safety concerns. An article that does not pass this review is revised or not published.

## Accountability

Technology providers are not credited as authors and are not responsible for published content. Responsibility remains with the named author and BriefBerlin LLC. Readers can question an editorial decision or report an error through our [Contact page]({{ '/contact/' | relative_url }}).
