Clara earned her Master's degree in **Deutsch als Zweitsprache (DaZ)**, German as a Second Language, from the **University of Leipzig**. During her time in Saxony, she specialized in immersive curriculum structures for adult learners and the cognitive pathways of bilingual students.

For Clara, teaching is more than a profession. The moment a challenging grammatical concept becomes clear to a student is what continues to drive her work.

At BriefBerlin, Clara covers Berlin local affairs, with a particular focus on educational policy, integration initiatives, and language programs. Her work connects academic theory with classroom practice and aims to give teachers, students, and families useful, research-informed context.

Outside writing and teaching, Clara hosts local language-exchange meetups and explores regional hiking trails.
